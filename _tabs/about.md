# About Roel Beijnes

Hello! I'm **Roel Beijnes**, a Senior Consultant for End User Computing at [Previder](https://www.previder.nl).

With over a decade of experience in application delivery technologies, I specialize in:

- **Application Packaging & Delivery**: MSI, App-V, ThinApp, VMware App Volumes
- **End User Computing Strategy & Operations**: Design and optimization of modern delivery strategies
